📚 Vocabulary & Word Knowledge

Build and reinforce vocabulary, analyze word relationships, understand morphology

Word Quest

Tier 1-3

Purpose: Practice and reinforce vocabulary through word clue solving

Science of Reading Domain: Vocabulary
Best For: Tier 1 whole-class vocabulary preview, Tier 2 small-group word analysis, Tier 3 intensive vocabulary support
Duration: 10-15 minutes
Group Size: Whole class (projection), small group (2-4), or individual
Curriculum Alignment: Fishtank ELA (vocabulary units) • Fundations (word families) • All core programs

Students solve word clues to guess hidden words. A classic, engaging vocabulary warm-up that reinforces prior knowledge and builds word consciousness. Includes options for word families, synonyms, and morphological analysis.

Tier 1-2

🎤 Speaking & Oral Language

Build articulation, fluency, and self-awareness through audio recording and feedback

Speaking Studio

Tier 1-3 NEW

Purpose: Record, listen, and visualize speech patterns for self-awareness

Science of Reading Domain: Oral Language (fluency, articulation, prosody)
Best For: EAL students • Speech support • Fluency practice • Self-monitoring
Recording Method: Local device only (no cloud upload)

Students record themselves speaking (reading fluency, pronunciation, articulation practice) and immediately hear playback. Visual waveforms show speech patterns. Compare to professional model audio. Build meta-awareness of own voice and speech patterns.

🎤 Local recording 🌊 Waveform visualization 👂 Playback comparison 🔒 Privacy-first
