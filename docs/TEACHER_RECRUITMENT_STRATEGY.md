## IDEAL TEACHER PROFILE

### Experience
- Reading specialist OR intervention specialist OR classroom teacher with intervention focus
- 0-15 years in role (mix of all levels valued)
- Evidence of self-directed learning (professional development, certifications)

### Context
- K-5 grade focus (primary testing domain)
- School with diverse student population
- Access to small group/1:1 intervention time
- Some student data/assessment results available

### Characteristics
- Growth mindset ("love to get feedback on new tools")
- Detail-oriented (will notice bugs, give specific feedback)
- Communicative (will respond to surveys, share honestly)
- Not looking for "perfect" tool (understands testing phase)

### Red Flags to Avoid
- "I'll try it if I have time" (uncommitted)
- Very high tech anxiety (won't engage with platform)
- Needs extensive hand-holding (consumes support resources)
- Under-resourced schools (can't dedicate even 15 min/day)

## SCRIPTS FOR PHONE CALLS

### Initial Principal Contact (2-3 min)

*"Hi [Principal Name], this is [Your Name] with [Organization]. I'm calling about a product development opportunity for [School Name]. We're building an MTSS platform with input from specialists, and I thought [School Name] might be interested in having [X] of your specialists test it over the next few weeks. Would you be open to a quick conversation about whether this fits your school?"*

**If yes**: Schedule 15-min call for later
**If hesitant**: "No pressure - let me send you an email with details, and you can pass it along to your reading specialist if interested."

### Direct Teacher Outreach (3-4 min)

*"Hi [Teacher Name], this is [Your Name]. I'm reaching out because you came recommended as someone who [description: innovative, detail-oriented, great teacher]. We're testing a new MTSS platform before wider launch, and I think you'd be a perfect fit. The commitment is pretty modest - 15-30 minutes a day for 2-3 weeks, mostly using the platform and sharing quick feedback. Are you interested in hearing more?"*

**If interested**: Share details, get email, send recruitment email
