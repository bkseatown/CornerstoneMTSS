**Audience**: Product leadership, educators, administrators, hiring committees

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The Cornerstone MTSS platform has significant foundational strengths:
- ✅ Sophisticated curriculum alignment system (Fishtank, IM, Fundations, UFLI, etc.)
- ✅ Science of Reading framework baked into the content
- ✅ MTSS risk band system for tiered intervention
- ✅ Professional teacher-facing workflow

**However**, the current game portfolio is **fragmented and underdeveloped**:
- 7 games of widely varying maturity (Word Quest at 1,410 lines vs. Typing Quest at 70 lines)
- Unclear pedagogical purpose for each game
- No obvious alignment to Science of Reading domains
- Gallery UX doesn't communicate WHY these games exist or HOW to use them

**Strategic Opportunity**: Redesign the game portfolio to be **fewer, deeper, more purposeful games** that are:
1. Grounded in Science of Reading research
2. Aligned to your curriculum system
3. Professional enough to show admins and hiring committees
4. Flexible across Tier 1 (whole class), Tier 2 (small group), Tier 3 (intensive)

## STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATION: THE "FOCUSED CORE" APPROACH

Instead of many underdeveloped games, invest in **3-4 flagship games**, each:
- **Deep**: 500+ lines of code, fully featured
- **Clear**: Obvious pedagogical purpose
- **Flexible**: Works across Tier 1, 2, and 3
- **Aligned**: Connected to your curriculum system
- **Professional**: Shows credibility to administrators

### Proposed Game Portfolio

#### 🎮 **Tier 1 Games** (Whole-class practice, universal support)

**1. WORD QUEST (Vocabulary & Word Analysis)**
- **Keep/Expand**: This is your flagship. It's ready.
- **Purpose**: Pre-teach and reinforce vocabulary, analyze word relationships, understand morphology
- **SoR Domain**: Vocabulary + Morphology
- **Curriculum Fit**: All core programs (Fishtank ELA, Fundations, UFLI)
- **Use Cases**:
  - Whole class vocabulary preview (Tier 1)
  - Small group word analysis (Tier 2)
  - Intensive individual vocabulary intervention (Tier 3)
- **Enhancement Ideas**:
  - Add morphology activities (prefixes, suffixes, roots) → connects to phonics AND vocabulary
  - Add word relationships (synonyms, antonyms, semantic fields) from your curriculum
  - Connection to Fundations scope/sequence if available
  - Export/print features for home practice

**2. FLUENCY STATION (Reading Fluency - NEW)**
- **Consolidate + Expand**: Merge Reading Lab + Typing Quest into ONE comprehensive fluency tool
- **Purpose**: Build oral reading fluency (accuracy, speed, prosody); support keyboarding automaticity
- **SoR Domain**: Fluency (reading fluency, automaticity)
- **Curriculum Fit**: All programs emphasize fluency benchmarks (WCPM, accuracy %)
- **Modalities**:
  - **Passage Reading**: Timed passage reading with accuracy/speed feedback (ORF assessment-aligned)
  - **Sentence Fluency**: Repeated reading of single sentences for expression
  - **Word Fluency**: High-frequency word automaticity (sight words)
  - **Typing Fluency**: Keyboarding practice with real text from curriculum
- **Use Cases**:
  - Whole class fluency practice (Tier 1)
  - Small group repeated reading (Tier 2)
  - Individual fluency intervention + monitoring (Tier 3)
- **Assessment Feature**: Progress monitoring aligned to WCPM benchmarks

**3. SENTENCE STUDIO (Sentence Construction & Editing - REVAMP)**
- **Keep/Expand**: Replace underdeveloped Sentence Surgery + Paragraph Builder
- **Purpose**: Explicit syntax/grammar instruction → composing and editing sentences, building toward paragraph writing
- **SoR Domain**: Writing (Sentence-level composition and editing)
- **Curriculum Fit**: Fishtank ELA, Fundations sentence activities
- **Game Modes**:
  - **Sentence Building**: Arrange words to form grammatically correct sentences (explicit syntax)
  - **Sentence Editing**: Fix grammar, capitalization, punctuation errors (error identification)
  - **Sentence Expansion**: Add adjectives, adverbs, prepositional phrases to base sentences
  - **Sentence Variation**: Rewrite sentences with different structures (complexity building)
- **Use Cases**:
  - Whole class grammar mini-lesson practice (Tier 1)
  - Small group writing intervention (Tier 2)
  - Intensive individual grammar/syntax support (Tier 3)

---

#### 🎮 **Tier 2/3 Games** (Targeted, intensive intervention)

**4. DECODING WORKSHOP (Phonics & Decoding - NEW/ENHANCED)**
- **Potential New Game**: Currently have Decoding Diagnostic (assessment), but NO interactive phonics game
- **Purpose**: Explicit, systematic phonics instruction → systematic application to decodable text
- **SoR Domain**: Phonics + Phonemic Awareness (critical Tier 2/3 intervention)
- **Curriculum Fit**: UFLI Foundations, Fundations (Wilson), your curriculum scope/sequence
- **Game Modes**:
  - **Letter-Sound Matching**: Build automaticity with grapheme-phoneme correspondence
  - **Word Blending**: Sound-by-sound blending of decodable words
  - **Word Families**: CVC families, CVCE, consonant blends
  - **Decodable Reading**: Apply phonics to simple decodable texts (progress monitoring)
  - **Syllable Patterns**: Compound words, multi-syllabic decoding
- **Use Cases**:
  - Small group phonics intervention (Tier 2)
  - Intensive 1:1 decoding remediation (Tier 3)
  - Progress monitoring aligned to UFLI/Fundations scope
- **Why This Matters**: **This is what's MISSING from your portfolio.** Decoding/phonics is foundational for Science of Reading, and you have no interactive game for it.

## GAME GALLERY REDESIGN (UX)

### Current Problem
Game gallery shows cards but doesn't teach users WHY each game exists or which to choose.

### Proposed Solution: **"By Purpose" Organization**

Instead of grid of game names, organize by:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ LITERACY INTERVENTION GAMES                         │
│ "Choose by what your students need to practice"     │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

📖 FLUENCY & ACCURACY
  ├─ Fluency Station (ORF, accuracy, expression)
  │  └─ "Time and measure reading speed + accuracy"
  │  └─ Works for: Tier 1 class-wide fluency, Tier 2 small group RRR
  │  └─ Aligns to: [WCPM Benchmark] [Grade Level] [DIBELS]

📚 WORD KNOWLEDGE & VOCABULARY
  ├─ Word Quest (Vocabulary, morphology, word relationships)
  │  └─ "Build and reinforce word knowledge"
  │  └─ Works for: Tier 1 vocab pre-teach, Tier 2 word analysis, Tier 3 intensive
  │  └─ Aligns to: [Fishtank ELA] [Fundations] [Your Word Bank]

✏️ WRITING & COMPOSITION
  ├─ Sentence Studio (Grammar, syntax, sentence building)
  │  └─ "Construct, edit, and expand sentences"
  │  └─ Works for: Tier 1 grammar practice, Tier 2 writing intervention
  │  └─ Aligns to: [Fishtank ELA writing tasks]

🔤 PHONICS & DECODING (Tier 2/3 Intensive)
  ├─ Decoding Workshop (Phonics, word families, decodable text)
  │  └─ "Systematic phonics instruction with immediate feedback"
  │  └─ Works for: Tier 2/3 intensive phonics intervention
  │  └─ Aligns to: [UFLI Foundations] [Fundations Scope] [Your Phonics Sequence]
```

### Key UX Improvements
- ✅ **Purpose-first**: Each game description starts with "What is this for?"
- ✅ **MTSS callout**: Clear indication of which tier(s) it supports
- ✅ **Curriculum alignment**: Shows connection to your systems (UFLI, Fundations, Fishtank, etc.)
- ✅ **Use case examples**: "When would a teacher use this?"
- ✅ **Implementation notes**: "How long? How many students? With what frequency?"
