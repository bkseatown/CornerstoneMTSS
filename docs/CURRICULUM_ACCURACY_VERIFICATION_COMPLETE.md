### Step Up To Writing
**Structure**: Grade bands (K-2, 3-5, 6-8, 9-12)  
**What Added**:
- K-2: Foundational skills, Narrative, Informative
- 3-5: Opinion/Argument, Narrative, Informative, Research
- 6-8: Argumentative, Informative, Narrative, Research Projects
- 9-12: Rhetorical Analysis, Essay, Research & Argumentation

**SWBAT Examples**:
- "Write opinion paragraphs with clear opinions, supporting reasons, and concluding sentences"
- "Write arguments with clear claims, supporting evidence, logical reasoning, and counterargument acknowledgment"

**Source**: https://www.voyagersopris.com/products/writing/step-up-to-writing/overview

### LLI (Leveled Literacy Intervention)
**Structure**: Guided Reading Levels A-Z with 8 level bands  
**What Added**:
- Levels A-B (Kindergarten)
- Levels C-E (Early Grade 1)
- Levels F-J (Grade 1)
- Levels K-N (Grade 2)
- Levels O-Q (Grade 2-3)
- Levels R-T (Grade 3-4)
- Levels U-W (Grade 4-5)
- Levels X-Z (Grade 5+)

**SWBAT Examples**:
- "Recognize individual letters and sounds; follow print with one-to-one match; read high-frequency words"
- "Use morpheme knowledge to decode complex words; read with expression at 100+ WPM; analyze text structure"

**Source**: https://www.fountasandpinnell.com/lli/

### SAS 9th Grade Humanities
**Structure**: Integrated ELA + World History with 6 modules  
**What Added**:
1. Ancient Civilizations (Egypt, Mesopotamia, Greece, Rome)
2. Medieval World (Europe, Islamic Golden Age, Africa/Asia)
3. Renaissance & Reformation (Intellectual/religious/scientific change)
4. Age of Exploration (Colonization, global trade impact)
5. Industrial Revolution (Technology, society, imperialism)
6. Contemporary Voices (Modern literature, human experiences)

**SWBAT Examples**:
- "Analyze how ancient civilizations developed complex societies; compare governmental systems"
- "Examine effects of colonization on indigenous populations; understand emergence of global commerce"

**Source**: https://www.sas.edu.sg/academics/curriculum-guides
