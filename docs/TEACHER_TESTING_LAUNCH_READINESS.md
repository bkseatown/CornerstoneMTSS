## DECISION FRAMEWORK

### Metrics to Track During Testing

| Metric | Red Flag | Yellow Flag | Green Flag | Ideal |
|--------|----------|------------|-----------|-------|
| **Daily Active Users** | <30% | 30-70% | >70% | >90% |
| **Feature Adoption** | <50% | 50-80% | >80% | >95% |
| **Satisfaction** | <4/10 | 4-6/10 | >6/10 | >8/10 |
| **Would Recommend** | <25% | 25-50% | >50% | >80% |
| **Recommendation Match** | <40% | 40-60% | >60% | >80% |
| **Critical Bugs** | >5 | 3-5 | <3 | 0 |
| **Support Burden** | >3 issues/day | 2-3/day | <2/day | <1/day |

### Go/No-Go Criteria

**GO (Proceed to Wider Launch)**
- ✅ >70% daily active users
- ✅ >80% core feature adoption
- ✅ Satisfaction >6/10
- ✅ <3 critical bugs
- ✅ Positive qualitative feedback
- ✅ Teachers want to continue using

**CONDITIONAL GO (Proceed with Known Issues)**
- ⚠️ 50-70% daily active users
- ⚠️ 60-80% feature adoption
- ⚠️ Known issues documented & workarounds provided
- ⚠️ Teachers see enough value to continue
- ⚠️ Plans to fix identified issues

**NO-GO (Pivot or Iterate)**
- ❌ <50% daily active users (engagement too low)
- ❌ >5 critical bugs (stability issues)
- ❌ Satisfaction <4/10 (product doesn't resonate)
- ❌ Recommendation engine confidence <40% match
- ❌ Teachers request fundamental changes

## RISK MITIGATION

### Risk: Low Teacher Recruitment
**Mitigation**:
- Start with known contacts + referrals
- Offer meaningful incentive
- Emphasize opportunity to shape product
- Have backup contact list ready

### Risk: Platform Bugs During Testing
**Mitigation**:
- Comprehensive smoke test before launch
- Support team ready to respond
- Quick deployment capability
- Known issues documented with workarounds

### Risk: Teachers Don't Engage
**Mitigation**:
- Clear expectations in recruitment
- Easy-to-use demo caseload
- Frequent touchpoints (surveys, emails, office hours)
- Incentive tied to participation

### Risk: Ambiguous Feedback
**Mitigation**:
- Structured surveys (not open-ended only)
- Quantitative + qualitative data
- Follow-up debrief calls for clarification
- Regular support team updates on sentiment

### Risk: Technical Issues Discovered
**Mitigation**:
- Quick escalation and fix process
- Honest communication with teachers
- Workarounds provided
- Issue adds to post-testing roadmap
