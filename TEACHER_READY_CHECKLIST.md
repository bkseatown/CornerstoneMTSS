## Deployment Instructions

### For Teachers
1. Navigate to `https://bkseatown.github.io/CornerstoneMTSS/word-quest.html`
2. Select grade and focus level
3. Click "Next Word" to begin
4. Students type letters (or use on-screen keyboard)
5. Press Enter to guess
6. Use spacebar or click "Play Again" for next word

### Classroom Best Practices
- **Whole Class**: Use projector with student typing
- **Small Group**: Multiple students take turns on one device
- **Individual**: Students play on tablets/laptops
- **Fluency Practice**: Quick rounds (2-5 min) as warm-up
- **Assessment**: Use hint/clue system to scaffold difficulty
